Newsletter Campaigns for Subscribers and Groups.

== Description ==

Newsletter Campaigns for Subscribers and [Groups](https://wordpress.org/plugins/groups/). Supports newsletter sign-ups at checkout, to let customers subscribe during checkout with WooCommerce.

== Installation ==

1. Go to *Plugins > Add New > Upload* and choose the plugin's zip file, or extract the contents and copy the `groups-newsletters` folder to your site's `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Enable the plugin from the *Plugins* menu in WordPress.
